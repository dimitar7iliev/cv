Dimitar Iliev

Actor and Stage Director

Dimitar Iliev (Bulgarian: Димитър Илиев) was born on April 23rd 1994 in Sofia, Bulgaria. Iliev's theatre career began during high school years, where he participated in Petar Varbanov's theatre company. Meanwhile, Iliev is a drummer in various rock bands. In 2014 he was enrolled as a student in the National Academy for Theatre and Film Arts ""Krastyo Sarafov"" in Sofia.The same year he made his debut as an actor on the stage of "Ivan Vazov" National Theatre with the role of Grozev in "Geo" by Hristo Karastoyanov. "Geo" won three ASKEER awards and two IKAR awards.
